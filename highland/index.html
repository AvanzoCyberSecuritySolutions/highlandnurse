ADMISSIONS

Join our prestigious nursing program and start your journey towards a rewarding healthcare career.

Admission Requirements

10+2 (Science Stream)

Applicants must have successfully passed their higher secondary education in the science stream.

NEET Qualification

A valid score in the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a mandatory requirement.

Document Submission

Submission of relevant academic certificates (10+2 marks card), NEET scorecard, and identity proof.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Is NEET qualification mandatory for admission?

Yes, NEET qualification is a mandatory requirement for B.Sc Nursing admission as per the latest guidelines.

Q2: Can I pay the fees in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options. You may contact the admissions office for a customized payment plan.

Q3: Is hostel accommodation available?

Details about hostel facilities and availability will be provided to confirmed students during the admission counseling process.

Q4: What documents are required at the time of application?

You will need to submit your NEET scorecard, 10+2 marks card, a valid government-issued ID proof, and recent passport-sized photographs.

Q5: When do classes start for the 2025 batch?

The academic calendar and official start date for the 2025 batch will be announced soon. Please stay updated through our website.
