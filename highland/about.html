Our Story

History & Background

Highland College of Nursing is a proud initiative of Mariam Educational Trust, which has been dedicated to the field of education for over two decades. The Trust is known for its unwavering commitment to academic excellence, value-based learning, and community development. The establishment of Highland College of Nursing marks a bold step forward in healthcare education, aiming to produce highly skilled and ethically grounded nursing professionals.

Our Mission

“Nurturing Competence with Compassion”

  • We aim to provide high-quality education and clinical training in nursing and allied health sciences, fostering a spirit of service and professional excellence.
  • We are committed to holistic student development through a curriculum that emphasizes evidence-based practice, innovation, and ethical care.
  • We promote a safe, inclusive, and intellectually stimulating environment that supports lifelong learning and leadership in healthcare.
  • Our mission is to bridge healthcare education with community impact, ensuring our graduates are equipped to serve with skill, empathy, and accountability.
  • TRANSPARENCY & TRUST – We uphold integrity in every process, ensuring that our students, faculty, and stakeholders experience fairness, clarity, and respect.

Our Vision

  • To be a distinguished center of excellence in health and allied sciences education, committed to innovation, inclusivity, and service.
  • To cultivate compassionate, competent, and ethical professionals ready to meet global healthcare challenges.
  • To establish an integrated academic environment where research, community service, and leadership development thrive together.
  • To lead the transformation of healthcare education by blending modern knowledge with values rooted in humanity and social responsibility.
  • To become a trusted hub for nursing and allied health sciences education in India and beyond.

Accreditation & Affiliations
